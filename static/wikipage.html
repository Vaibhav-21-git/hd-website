First Unit - 140A (LEASE SIGNED)

This space is both a) amazing like a dream and b) over budget.  Huge open space plus offices in multiple levels, multiple bathrooms, locking rooms, extra storage space, an amazing entrace with a mini lobby, and has lots of character.  The previous owners made glass designs, so there are stained glass doors and all kinds of neat stuff.  The picture at the top of the page is the main entrace to 140A alone.
